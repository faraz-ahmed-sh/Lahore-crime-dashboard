Lahore Crime Rate: Unprecedented in 2014


Significant amount of resources have been spent in preventing crime in Lahore over the past few years.
However, most of these efforts have been directed to prevent crime in a certain neighborhood and thus fail to recognize other aspects
of crime. What type of crimes occur where? Do some types of crime such as robbery and murder occur more frequently in certain time periods?
Through this project, I aim to shed light on some of these aspects for better policing decision-making.
